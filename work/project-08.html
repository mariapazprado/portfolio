ESPACIO RAÍZ

Academic Project Multipurpose Hall | TFM MDAi 2022

A satellite space for the "Scala Naturae" hotel within a 600 m² industrial warehouse. Includes workshops for organic products (from local species), an interactive tourist center, showrooms and tastings of products sold and used in the hotel. The interaction with each specie will depend on its characteristic: aromatic (smell), edible (taste), textile (touch). To accomplish this, the historic Bourbon orchards are restored and cultivated, supplying the raw materials for production while simultaneously promoting local tourism.
