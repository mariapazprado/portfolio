ALCALA STARTUP

Academic Project Co-living space | MDAi 2022

Alcalá 141 Building in Madrid is a project designed by De Lapuerta + Campo Arquitectos, its 4th floor was the basis for this exercise. The brief was to create a flexible space where a diverse group of people can carry out different activities.


Alcalá Start-up is a collaborative space created for individuals or groups to connect and enhance the creative process.


The main space is divided into movable modules. The use of high-resistance, low-impact materials helps with maintenance.
