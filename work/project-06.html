ALCALA STARTUP

Academic Project Co-living space | MDAi 2022

The Alcalá 141 building in Madrid, designed by De Lapuerta + Campo Arquitectos, provided the fourth floor as the basis for this exercise. The brief called for a flexible space where diverse groups of users can carry out a range of activities.


Alcalá Start‑Up is a collaborative environment where individuals and groups can connect and enhance the creative process.


The main area is organized into movable modules; using durable, low‑impact materials improves longevity and reduces maintenance needs.
